Vault Hunter Upper Body Blast

Inspired by: Lilith

A punchy upper body workout designed to burn and build, inspired by the firehawk herself

Difficulty: Medium

  • Dynamic Arm Circles: 2 x 10 each way
  • Push-Ups: 3 x 12
  • Inverted Rows or Bent-Over Rows: 3 x 10
  • Overhead Press (dumbbells or bands): 3 x 8
  • Tricep Dips: 3 x 10
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 x 30 sec
  • Cooldown: Stretch chest, shoulders, triceps
