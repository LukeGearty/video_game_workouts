Geralt's Witcher Training

Inspired by: Geralt of Rivia

Mobility, raw strength, and combat readiness like a monster-slaying legend

Difficulty: Hard

  • Warm-Up Flow: World's Greatest Stretch + Arm Swings – 3 minutes
  • Deadlifts (or backpack deadlifts): 4 x 5
  • Lunges with Rotation: 3 x 8 each side
  • Push-Up to Pike: 3 x 10
  • Pull-Ups or Towel Rows: 3 x max reps
  • Core Finisher: Hollow Body Hold – 3 x 30 sec
  • Cooldown: Deep hip openers + breathing
