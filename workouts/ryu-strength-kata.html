Ryu Strength Kata

Inspired by: Ryu

Discipline-focused strength, balance, and explosive power for martial arts conditioning

Difficulty: Medium

  • Warm-Up: Dynamic stretches + deep breathing
  • Horse Stance Hold: 3 x 30 sec
  • Push-Ups: 4 x 10
  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 x 15
  • Reverse Lunges with Knee Drive: 3 x 10 each leg
  • Jumping Front Kicks (air): 3 x 10 each leg
  • Plank Hold with Fists: 3 x 45 sec
  • Cool Down: Slow breathing + static stretches
