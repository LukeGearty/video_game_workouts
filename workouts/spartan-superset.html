Spartan Superset

Inspired by: Master Chief

Heavy strength and endurance training worthy of a Spartan-II supersoldier

Difficulty: Hard

  • Warm-Up: 5 mins dynamic full-body mobility
  • Superset 1: Deadlifts + Push-Ups – 3 x (5 Deadlifts + 15 Push-Ups)
  • Superset 2: Pull-Ups + Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 x (10 Pull-Ups + 8 each leg)
  • Superset 3: Plank Rows + Jump Squats – 3 x (10 Rows + 10 Jumps)
  • Farmer's Carries: 3 rounds, 30 meters
  • Cooldown: 5 mins stretching and breath work
