Power Suit Workout

Inspired by: Samus Aran

Agility, core strength, and explosive movement like the intergalactic bounty hunter herself

Difficulty: Medium

  • 20 Jump rope or simulated rope hops
  • 10 Arm circles
  • 10 Inchworm walkouts
  • 10 Glute Bridges
  • 10 Lateral lunges
  • 5 Burpees
  • V-ups or tuck crunches: 20 reps
  • banded punches or explosive push ups: 15 each arm
  • inverted rows or bent-over rows: 12 reps
  • jump squats: 15 reps
  • jumping lunges or step-back lunges: 10 each leg
  • plank with alternating shoulder taps: 1 minute
