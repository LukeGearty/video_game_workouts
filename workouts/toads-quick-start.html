Toad's Quick Start

Inspired by: Toad

A short, low-impact routine for boosting energy without exhausting your mushrooms

Difficulty: Easy

  • March in Place: 2 x 30 sec
  • Shoulder Rolls: 2 x 10 each way
  • Wall Sits: 2 x 20 sec
  • Toe Touches: 2 x 15
  • Standing Calf Raises: 2 x 12
  • Arm Swings: 2 x 15
  • Cool Down: Gentle walk or seated breathing
