Stealth Assassin Workout

Inspired by: Solid Snake

Functional strength, stealthy mobility, and endurance under pressure

Difficulty: Medium

  • Bear Crawls: 3 x 20 sec
  • Push-Ups: 3 x 12
  • Bodyweight Rows: 3 x 10
  • Step-Ups: 3 x 10 each leg
  • Wall Sits: 3 x 45 sec
  • Plank: 3 x 60 sec
  • Side Lunges: 3 x 10 each side
  • Cool Down: Forward fold, pigeon stretch, deep squat hold
